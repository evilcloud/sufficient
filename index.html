Beyond the blade

A fit for projects that don't fit in

Not all projects are mission-critical. Not all projects fit the blade server either. We are taking a niche of non-standard colocation, hosting and computations that fits the projects, that don’t fit anywhere else.

What does the whole thing looks like

Sufficient Networks has enough vertical infrastructure to sustain a Tier I level demand that typically is done only as a highly custom product with the big data centres or hosting companies. The access is provided according to the requirements. Furthermore, the cost is transparent and supported with SNMP reports.

This is not a webhosting company. There is no 99.9% uptime guarantee—plenty of services are better services doing this. We excel at providing customized, tailored solutions for unusual problems. Honestly, our preferred method is to hack something together that will work right there and right then. While this approach is often frowned upon in most circles, it may be just something what a short-term project needs. It was the last mile of PM.

What we offer

Colocation

Not all projects require a blade server. Many won't even fit into a standard rack. We provide a purpose-set secure floor space, with high speed connection, and sufficient electricity to supply your machine.

Computation power

Need that extra kick for the render? Or perhaps run the blocks to secure the chain? We have just sufficient power to do that. Focus at the task at hand, instead of building infrastructure just for a short mission.

Access

Clean IP with no history of bad behavour, VPN, CND, SSH and SNMP. We offer the full scale of industry standards with no drama, no overheads and with a full transparency.

The essence of sufficency

there is no doubt that striving for the best is an admirable ambition and we all should makie it our priority when we want to achieve the greatness. Sometimes, however, the best is not the most effient. Perfectionism is known in psychological circles as one of the symptoms to an unhealthy behaviour. Depending on the project just enough is exactly what is neccesary, whether the constrain is financial, temporal or simply the importance of the step is not at the top priority.

Infrastructure

We are not a multi-billion dollar datacenter built by publicly listed corporation - our facilities have sufficient space, power and connection for most short-term projects. Just don't try to lauch a rocket with our help, please.

Platform

Our facilities are platform-agnostic and we are flexible to your requirements. You run whatever hardware or software you want and we'll charge you for traffic, power and management as agreed. There are no hidden costs and the only limitations are your imagination and our technical abilities.

Personal

The small scale allows us to approach your matters personally. There are no tickets online, each case is dealt with individually and you are in direct contact with the responsible person, not an operator.
